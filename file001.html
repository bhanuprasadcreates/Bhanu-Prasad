FILE 001

ARCHIVED REPORT

Gaon ke bahar, jungle ke paas, ek purani haveli thi jiske baare me log sirf itna kehte the — “Wahan koi rehta hai… par dikhta nahi.”

Haveli band nahi thi. Gate hamesha adha khula rehta tha. Lekin ajeeb baat ye thi ki koi bhi andar jaakar wapas nahi aata tha.

Police records me haveli ka naam kai baar badla gaya, lekin har report me ek cheez same thi:

Last known activity — 02:17 AM

Us raat kisi ne cheekhne ki awaaz nahi suni. Kisi ne bhaagte hue kadam nahi dekhe. Bas… lights ek ke baad ek band hoti chali gayi.

Is report ke baad, haveli se judi kuch files ko permanently seal kar diya gaya.
